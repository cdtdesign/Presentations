WOW Balls at Central Florida Zoo ACTIVE!
Travelers, take a "walk on water" with the WOW Balls at the Central Florida Zoo in Sanford, FL. Keep dry and take a 5 minute spin inside these fun spheres before checking out the great animals inside the zoo. The Central Florida Zoo is conveniently located just off of I-4 in Sanford at 3755 NW Hwy 17-92, Sanford, FL and is open from 9AM to 5PM all year round, closed only for Thanksgiving and Christmas.
